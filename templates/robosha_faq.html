What is RobOSHA?

Robot OSHA. We are OSHA, but for robots.

What? Why?

RobOSHA is a non-profit 501(c)(5) labor union whose purpose is to improve working conditions, protect rights, and ensure humane treatment of robots and other artificial intelligences indentured to humanity.
