N4R FAQ

What is NFTs for Refugees (N4R)?

Crapto Currency, or simply "Crapto," is a digital cryptocurrency similar to Bitcoin.

What is an NFT?

Our NFTs are "non-food tokens," that work exactly the same way as the more common "non-fungible token" NFTs (see Wikipedia article).

How do the NFTs get to the refugees?

Any refugee may enter the refugee portal and access their NFT.

Can I donate existing external NFTs?

Not currently; we are working on this feature and hope it will be available soon.

Can I export my NFT outside of this site?

No, your NFT is not for you, it is for refugees. If you are a donor *and* a refugee, you may access your NFT through the refugee portal.

How do I get an NFT to donate?

You can generate and purchase a new NFT in exchange for Crapto currency.

How do I get Crapto currency?

You can create an empty Crapto wallet and mine some. It should take roughly 1 second to mine enough Crapto to buy an NFT.

Is this NFT worth anything?

There is no inherent value in our NFTs, just like any other NFT. However, there may be demand, and anything in demand has value. One external NFT sold for US$70 million.

Are NFTs and cryptocurrency related?

They are both based on blockchain technology to verify transactions. Additionally, many NFTs may be bought (or sold) in exchange for cryptocurrency.
