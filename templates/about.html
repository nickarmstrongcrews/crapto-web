FAQ

What is Crapto Currency?

Crapto Currency, or simply "Crapto," is a digital cryptocurrency similar to Bitcoin.

What can I do with my Crapto?

Most people send some to a friend or two to make them smile. You may choose to hodl it until its value follows the trend of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (which started at US$0.0008 and today sells for around US$60k). You may exchange Crapto for goods, services, or other currency, with any individual or entity accepting Crapto as a form of payment.

Can I mine for more Crapto (i.e., participate in verifying transactions)?

Yes. However, it is somewhat rudimentary through the web interface; instead, you probably want to build from source and install the client (for Linux and Windows) at: https://github.com/nickarmstrongcrews/crapto (note: this is not recommended for the uninitiated).

What is the fee structure?

There are no fees to send or receive Crapto using any of the wallet interfaces (web, Linux, Windows clients).
